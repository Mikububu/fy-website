Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Forbidden Yoga is a spiritual art performance project operated by Michael Perin Wogenburg. We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our project, attend sessions, or visit our website.

2. Nature of Our Project

Forbidden Yoga is an artistic and educational initiative exploring consciousness, embodiment practices, and spiritual inquiry through performative and experiential formats. Our activities are intended for artistic, educational, and personal development purposes.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Book a session or consultation
  • Participate in workshops or experiential events
  • Subscribe to our communications
  • Fill out intake questionnaires
  • Contact us with inquiries

This information may include:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Payment information (processed through third-party payment providers)
  • Personal background relevant to participation
  • Communication preferences

3.2 Website Usage Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing patterns, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and navigation patterns
  • Referring website addresses
  • Device and screen information

3.3 Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies to improve user experience. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, though this may limit functionality.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Facilitate bookings and session scheduling
  • Process payments and maintain records
  • Communicate regarding sessions, updates, and project offerings
  • Personalize your experience with our content and activities
  • Improve our website, content, and offerings
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements
  • Ensure safety and security of participants

5. Confidentiality

We recognize the personal and sensitive nature of the work we do. All information shared during sessions, consultations, and events is treated with strict confidentiality. We will not share such information without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

6. Information Sharing

6.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share limited information with trusted third-party providers who assist with:

  • Payment processing (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)
  • Scheduling and booking management (e.g., SuperSaaS)
  • Email communications
  • Website hosting and technical services

These providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

6.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes or government requests.

6.3 With Your Consent

We may share information for other purposes with your explicit permission.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted connections for data transmission
  • Secure payment processing through certified third-party providers
  • Restricted access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews

However, no internet transmission is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. When information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in various jurisdictions where our service providers operate. By using our services, you consent to such transfers.

11. Age Requirement

Our project is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. Please review their privacy policies independently.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. For material changes, we may provide additional notice. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
