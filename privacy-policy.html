Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

Forbidden Yoga is deeply committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the highest standards of confidentiality. We understand that individuals seeking transformational tantric work require absolute discretion.

Information We Collect

When you inquire about our Sensual Liberation Retreats, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information
  • Information you choose to share in initial consultations
  • Communication preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries about retreats and offerings
  • Facilitate retreat bookings and logistics
  • Provide you with information about Forbidden Yoga programs

Confidentiality

We never share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties. All communications and participant information are treated with complete confidentiality. Your participation in Forbidden Yoga programs remains private.

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the inquiry form on our website.
