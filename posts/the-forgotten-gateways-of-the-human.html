The Forgotten Gateways of the Human Body

Why Forbidden Yoga is not about nudity or modern Tantra but about remembering the ancient current that awakens the full spectrum of consciousness.

Nov 05, 2025

At Forbidden Yoga we work with ancient yogic technologies that view the human body as a portal to knowledge and transformation. Our practice may at times include nudity or sexual energy, yet its essence is not about either of these. It is about opening a stream that reveals how a human can feel in its most optimal state of consciousness. In other words, it brings us back to the original purpose, awakening the current of awareness that flows through every layer of being. In the Shakta Tantra lineage we preserve, certain practices once used the body's exposed energy as a key to enter a vast hologram of humanity, a field where consciousness, emotion, and matter unite in sacred architecture. Forbidden Yoga reopens that current, translating these forgotten sciences into a living experience of clarity, strength, and wonder.
