ONLINE STUDY - A Forbidden Yoga Lineage

The Andhakaara Path to Power - An Epic Journey to the Source

Nov 25, 2023

The Andhakaara Path to Power (APTP) stands as a monumental 365-day Work Study training program, promising to transport its dedicated students into the realm of ancient Tantric Laya Yoga, Kriya Yoga, and a profound lineage of magical Tantric traditions. Yet, the program is famously defined by a striking irony: despite its extensive curriculum and spiritual ambitions, only one person is considered to have truly mastered the APTP in its totality.

This sole “yogini”, who took on the long project after suggesting such an endeavor would be impossible for anyone to complete in consecutive order, stands as the unique benchmark of success. This leaves an open challenge for all aspirants: “one so to say in the world has mastered the APTP... You want to be the second one?”

This self-imposed barrier highlights the depth and commitment required for the course, where even a single day’s practice is described as offering enough spiritual insight to last a lifetime.
